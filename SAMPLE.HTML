1.Department of Civil Engineering

Welcome to the Department of Civil Engineering at VR Siddhartha. We embarked on our journey in 1977 and over the last four decades, we have grown our expertise and competence in the core curriculum of Civil Engineering and research. We have a robust undergraduate program in Civil Engineering and allow our students to do Minor degree in Computer Science Engineering or Information Technology or Electronics and Communication Engineering. At the postgraduate level, we offer M. Tech. degree in Structural Engineering. The number of sanctioned students for B. Tech., and M. Tech. SE. are 120, and 18, respectively, per year. The Department of Computer Applications was established in the year 2004-2005 to run the three years Postgraduate programme in Master of Computer Applications(MCA) with excellent infrastructure and experienced faculty. About

Siddhartha Academy of General & Technical Education, Vijayawada, Andhra Pradesh
Siddhartha Academy, the Fountain head Siddhartha Academy of General and Technical Education had been formed in 1975 by a team of 250 philanthropists with a vision for future to set up and promote such educational institutions of excellence that would reorient general, technical and professional education with holistic approach. Assimilating the advances made in various domains of knowledge and considering the educational needs of the region, 15 academic institutions from KG to PG which includes Public schools(2), Arts & Science colleges(3), Engineering colleges(2), Pharmacy college, Medical college, Dental college, School of Nursing, College of Nursing, Law college, College of Education, hotel Management & Catering Technology and a couple of Service & Cultural organizations with 3,000 teaching & non teaching staff and 23,500 students have been established. In addition, the Academic has been gloriously rendering services to the Indian Heritage & Culture through Siddhartha Kalapeetham and supporting the deserving poor students though Siddhartha Foundation.
